Family Lounge

Spaces should reflect the right ambiance for the desired function. They are not only the assembly of mere structure, fenestration or supportive services but the spatial design should be qualitatively appropriate to induce a psycho-physical comfort, enlivening phenomenon with positive energy inputs among its users. In this light, a family lounge is designed where you can interact with your close family members, the all-time inspiration of your life. A space measuring 9m x 9m x 4.5m is offered for the same purpose.
