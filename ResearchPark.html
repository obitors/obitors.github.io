Erevna Research Park

The task was to design a residential apartment complex to accommodate the visiting researchers in IIT Kharagpur. The spaces are so conceived that it encompasses all the facilities to ensure a comfortable stay in the campus. The residential facility primarily consists of studio apartments, having both single occupancy and double occupancy facility. This project aims at dealing with the design considering the optimum solutions offered at a climatic and environmental level.
